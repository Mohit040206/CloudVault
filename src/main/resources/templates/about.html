About CloudVault

CloudVault is a secure and user-friendly platform designed to simplify the way you store, manage, and access your important documents. Built with modern technologies, it ensures both convenience and data safety.

Tech Stack

CloudVault is powered by:

  • Backend: Java 21, Spring Boot, Spring Security
  • Database: MySQL
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3 (Thymeleaf templates)

The Idea Behind CloudVault

The inspiration for CloudVault came during our very first year of college. Four of us – me, Amit, Priyanshu, and Sumaskant – dreamed of creating a place where we could store and relive our golden college memories together. Not just documents, but also photos and videos of our college life, safely stored in one platform.

Our vision was simple yet powerful: instead of public shareable links, access would be controlled through IDs and passwords. That way, only the people who truly shared those memories could upload, view, and download them — making it feel more personal, more private, and more connected.

What began as a casual discussion among friends has now taken shape as CloudVault — a project built with Java and Spring Boot, starting with document storage but with the future goal of expanding to images and videos that can be viewed and played directly inside the platform.

Future & Scope

While currently focused on secure document management, CloudVault aims to expand into supporting media files such as photos and videos. The long-term vision is to create a personal digital memory space where users can preserve and relive their journeys with friends, family, and colleagues.
